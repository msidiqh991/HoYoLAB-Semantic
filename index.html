The upcoming version 3.0 will introduce a vast rainforest and playable elemental character Dendro along with the arrival of the Wisdom Nation! Global interactive entertainment brand HoYoverse has announced that its Open World ARPG game, Genshin Impact, will release a new update v3.0 "Dawn of a Thousand Roses" on August 24th ....

This expansion includes not only Sumeru, the fourth of the seven nations, but also the Dendro elemental gameplay mechanism which is the final part of Genshin Impact's seven-element system. Optimizations, more features as well as massive expansions will also be implemented to enhance the gaming experience. Three new pieces of equipment, The Boon of the Elder Tree, Ultra-Hot Burner Lamp Test Model and Fishing Line Stabilizer, can be obtained and used to make collecting wood, processing food and fishing easier. A colored shadow overlay has also been applied to make Teyvat a world more alive.

In Phase 2 of the Banner 4.0 schedule we have brought back the Geo 5 Star Gentry of Hermitage Zhongli Banner and returned the Hydro Tartaglia (Childe) 5 Star Snezhnaya Farewell Banner.
